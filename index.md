### [Hyperbolic Disc Embedding of Functional Human Brain Connectomes]
**"Geometric representation of the brain's functional network"**

This study proposes a novel method to embed human brain functional connectomes into a hyperbolic space. By utilizing **hyperbolic disc embedding**, we revealed the hierarchical organization and core-periphery structure of resting-state fMRI networks, offering new insights into brain topology.

* [cite_start]**Published in:** *Network Neuroscience* (2022) [cite: 43, 44]
* [cite_start]**Key Findings:** Revealed characteristic functional cores using k-core percolation[cite: 42].
